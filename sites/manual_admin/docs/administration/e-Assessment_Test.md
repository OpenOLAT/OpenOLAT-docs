## Tab Correction  {: #tab_correction}

Administrators can choose whether to enable the grading tool for anonymous grading.

In addition, you can generally specify whether a grade will be released and made visible to students after a manual correction.

## Correction workflow tab {: #tab_correction-workflow}

Grading can also be performed by external graders. (Although they are registered in OpenOlat, they are not members of the course.) The grading workflow is available to them if it is enabled here.

Emails are needed to coordinate corrections made by multiple proofreaders. Pre-written email templates can be created in different languages for the various stakeholders.

The correction work that has been performed can be made available to various roles.
